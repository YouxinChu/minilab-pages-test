FAQ

Common questions

How does it work without a bot joining my meeting?
MeetingMind captures audio through a local desktop app — no visible bot joins your Zoom, Teams, or Meet call. Participants won't even know it's running unless you tell them.
Is it compliant with recording consent laws?
Yes. MeetingMind provides configurable consent notifications and supports all-party consent requirements. You control when and how participants are informed.
What platforms are supported?
Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. We integrate with Slack, email, Notion, and Jira for action item delivery.
How accurate is the transcription?
95%+ accuracy across English accents and dialects, with speaker identification. We use state-of-the-art speech models fine-tuned for meeting contexts.

Absolutely. The Free plan gives you 5 meetings per month with full features. The Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.
